About yogapose.app

Yogapose is an AI-powered asana assistant that uses computer vision to evaluate asanas / yoga postures in an uploaded image. The goal is not to replace a yoga teacher but rather to fill in the gap during an individuals practice when a teacher might not be present. This is a personal project.

Your data is not sold or shared with third parties and you always have the option to delete your images and/or your account at all time.

The image processing model used here, uses the dataset from Yoga-82: A New Dataset for Fine-grained Classification of Human Poses [2020] by M.Verma, S. Kumawat, Y. Nakashima and S. Raman in IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Workshops.
