About yogapose.app

This is a personal and hobby website (no commercial purposes) to encourage interest yoga worldwide.

We do not sell your data to third parties and you always have the option to delete your images and/or your account at all time.

The image processing model used here, uses the dataset from Yoga-82: A New Dataset for Fine-grained Classification of Human Poses [2020] by M.Verma, S. Kumawat, Y. Nakashima and S. Raman in IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Workshops.
